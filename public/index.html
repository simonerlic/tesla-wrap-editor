HOW TO USE YOUR WRAP

  1. Save your downloaded PNG to a USB drive
  2. Create a folder on the USB called Wraps
  3. Place your PNG file inside the Wraps folder
  4. Plug the USB into your Tesla
  5. Open: Toybox → Paint Shop → Wraps tab
  6. Select your custom wrap and enjoy!
